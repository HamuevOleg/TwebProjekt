Welcome to Moldova’s Wine Country

Discover the hidden gem of European winemaking. Moldova, with its rich viticultural heritage and stunning landscapes, offers an unforgettable journey through world-class wineries and unique wine tours. Explore, taste, and enjoy!

1. About Moldova’s Wine Culture

Moldova is home to some of the oldest wine traditions in the world, dating back over 5,000 years...

  • Over 140 wineries
  • Largest underground wine cellars in the world (Cricova & Milestii Mici)
  • Unique grape varieties: Feteasca Neagră, Feteasca Albă, Rara Neagră

2. Top Wineries to Visit

1. Cricova Winery - Famous for its vast underground cellars and premium wines.

2. Castel Mimi - A historic winery producing high-quality wines since 1893.

3. Milestii Mici - Home to the world’s largest underground wine collection.

3. Wine Tours & Experiences

  • Classic Wine Tour - Visit Cricova & Milestii Mici.
  • Premium Wine Tour - Private tastings with a sommelier.
  • Custom Wine Tour - Tailored experiences for private groups.

4. Wine Festivals & Events

  • National Wine Day (October) – Celebrating Moldovan wine culture.
  • Underland Wine & Music Festival – Underground event at Cricova.
  • Wine Vernissage – Showcasing new releases from Moldovan winemakers.
